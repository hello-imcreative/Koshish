Our Vision

A logical initiative by the students of Kamla Nehru Institute of Technology, Sultanpur, that has the potential of brightening the future of hundreds of poor children living in the villages nearby by providing them good and meaningful motivation for education...

Our Motto :-

1. Increase access and encourage enrollment to basic primary education, through community mobilization, for all children not in school.

2. Provide high quality relevant education through KOSHISH learning centres.

3. Encourage and enable each student to become an independent thinker and self learner.

4. Make learning joyful.

5. Help transform the community.

True to its Hindi literal, ‘KOSHISH’, an attempt of technocrats in Sultanpur, is mainly focused on providing free education to the marginalized kids, at least up to the level where they too can dream big. We try to nurture the zeal of enthusiasm in the kids. We cherish every moment we spend among them. We work for the overall development of the children, so they can find and follow their own way whenever they want. Every child we have a chance to deal with a dormant spark waiting to be kindled. Believing in this philosophy, KOSHISH was started by some enthusiastic members of the KNIT as a free tuition classes for underprivileged children.
